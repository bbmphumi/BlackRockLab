OUR SERVICES VISION.

The Geotechnical Division of Blackrocklab offers a full range of geotechnical testing services ranging from Building and road centreline investigation we also offer highly specialized testing carried out at our Laboratory. The company is also involved with major geotechnical and soils investigations and materials surveys.

Gravel and Soil Testing Services

Blackrocklab offers the full range of tests required for the Civil Engineering environment as far as soils and gravels are concerned. This includes tests on aggregate ranging from basic sieve analysis tests to highly sophisticated tests such as the durability mill index tests as well as stabilization designs including chemical and bitumen stabilizations.

Concrete Testing Services

Blackrocklab offers the full range of concrete testing and concrete related services including concrete mix designs. Backrocklab have experience in concrete construction projects requiring state of the art concrete mix design technologies and innovative experiments on different mix design approaches. Blackrocklab also carry out tests on cement and cement related products.

Field testing Services

Blackrock offers a field testing service consisting of several field teams conducting onsite testing and sampling such as density testing and DCP testing. The company furthermore provides services to carry out small to very large soil and geotechnical investigations including, sampling, profiling, testing and reporting.
